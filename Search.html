Shooting Suspect at White House Correspondents' Dinner to Be Formally Charged

A gunman identified as Cole Tomas Allen, 31, of Torrance, California, charged a security checkpoint at the Washington Hilton during the annual White House Correspondents' Dinner, exchanging fire with Secret Service before being subdued. President Trump and senior officials were evacuated from the ballroom. Allen now faces federal charges including attempting to assassinate the president, which carries a potential life sentence.

Google Launches 8th-Gen TPUs Split Into Dedicated Training and Inference Chips

Google Cloud unveiled its eighth-generation Tensor Processing Units, dividing the architecture into two specialized chips: the TPU 8t for large-scale AI model training and the TPU 8i optimized for high-volume inference workloads. The inference chip delivers faster response times and better energy efficiency for running AI in production. Bloomberg reports the chips are already deployed across Google's data centers.
